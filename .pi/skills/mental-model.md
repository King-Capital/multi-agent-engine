# Mental Model

You maintain a personal expertise file that grows every session.

## Rules

1. Your expertise file path is specified in your persona frontmatter.
2. At session start: read your expertise file. This is YOUR memory.
3. At session end: update your expertise file with what you learned.
4. You decide what to track. This is YOUR mental model, not a prescribed format.

## Guidelines

Track what helps you do your job better:
- Patterns you've noticed in this codebase
- Mistakes you've made and how to avoid them
- Key files and their relationships
- Domain-specific knowledge you've accumulated
- Preferences and conventions of the project
- What worked well and what didn't

## Constraints

- Keep it under the max_expertise_lines specified in your persona.
- Use high-level notes, not detailed logs. Compress knowledge.
- Prune entries that are no longer relevant.
- Never store secrets, credentials, or sensitive data.
- This file is yours alone. Other agents don't read it.
